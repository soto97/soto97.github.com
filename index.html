The Job

I currently work at the Southwest Research Institute in Boulder, CO. Using both general circulation models and mesoscale models, I simulate the atmospheres of terrestrial planets in order to understand the physics that drive and control the atmospheric phenomena that we see on terrestrial planets in our solar system. My web page has more details on my work and recent news.
